Our History

Founded in 2002 in Duckville.

Mr McDuck decided he was going to mass market his new bathtime bud that he enjoyed so much in the bath.

The duckling's of Duckville thought is a quacking idea and the rest as they say is history.

Our Process.

We manufacture our ducks out of 100% recycled plastics and use only the most eco and duckling friendly dyes and paints.

This ensures that your little duckies are safe even when exploring with their beaks and wings.
